About

Hope For Life Ministry is a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming the lives of Rwandan street children by equipping them with the resources necessary to leave street life and reintegrate back into society.

HFLM will be hosting a benefit dinner at the Edmonds Conference Center on October 12th from 6:00pm to 8:30pm. Join us for a semi-formal night to help end child homelessness in Rwanda and hear about our transformative work.

The night will begin with appetizers and a silent auction, where you will have the opportunity to win Sounders and Silvertips tickets, Rwandan crafts, or a luxurious 2 week stay in France (to name a few)! Enjoy an authentic Asian Cuisine followed by a presentation from HFLM’s three Co-Founders: Hilliary Anderson, Megan Swanson-Chao, and Chantal Umutesi (who is flying in from Rwanda!).
